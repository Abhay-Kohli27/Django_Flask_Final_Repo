Product Specifications

  • Type: Hass Avocado
  • Origin: California, USA
  • Certifications: USDA Organic, Non-GMO
  • Storage: Room temperature until ripe, then refrigerate
  • Ripening: Place in a paper bag with a banana to speed up ripening
  • Size: Medium (150-170g each)

Description

Our premium organic Hass avocados are locally grown in California using sustainable farming practices. These creamy avocados are hand-picked at peak maturity to ensure optimal ripening and flavor development. Rich in healthy monounsaturated fats, fiber, and nutrients, they're not just delicious but also nutritious.

These avocados typically arrive firm and ripen within 2-3 days at room temperature. Once ripe, they can be refrigerated to extend freshness for up to a week. For faster ripening, place them in a paper bag with a banana or apple.

Flavor Profile

Hass avocados are known for their rich, nutty flavor and buttery texture. The flesh is creamy with a subtle sweetness that complements both savory and spicy dishes.

Culinary Uses

Perfect for classic guacamole, avocado toast, salads, sandwiches, smoothies, or simply enjoyed with a sprinkle of salt and a squeeze of lime. Their creamy texture also makes them ideal for dips, spreads, and even baking.

Benefits

  • High in heart-healthy monounsaturated fats
  • Rich source of dietary fiber
  • Contains potassium, vitamin K, vitamin E, and B vitamins
  • Provides antioxidants like lutein and zeaxanthin
  • Supports healthy digestion and nutrient absorption

Sustainability

Our partner farms use water-efficient irrigation systems, natural pest management, and organic fertilization practices to minimize environmental impact. We're committed to supporting local farmers who prioritize sustainable agriculture.
