Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, delivery, and products.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to all major cities and surrounding suburbs within 30 miles of our distribution centers. Enter your zip code on our website to check if we deliver to your area.

How long does delivery take?

Our standard delivery time is within 1-2 hours of placing your order. You can also schedule deliveries up to 7 days in advance at a time that's convenient for you.

What if I'm not satisfied with my order?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with any product, simply contact our customer service within 24 hours of delivery, and we'll refund your purchase or replace the items.

Do you offer organic options?

Yes! We have a wide selection of certified organic produce, dairy, meat, and pantry items. Look for the "Organic" tag on our products when shopping.
